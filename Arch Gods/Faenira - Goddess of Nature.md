# Domains
Nature, plants, animals, the wild, decay, wonder, frivolity, freedom, natural beauty

# Alignment
Chaotic Good

# Name
Faenira. 
Mother Nature, 

# Appearance & Depiction
Depicted as a beautiful but fierce female elf with intricate antlers. She is usually wearing robes grown from plants. She has long red hair and piercing blue eyes.
